REGIME_DETECTION_V3

This module classifies current market conditions into a trading regime.

It does NOT generate trades.
It ONLY defines context for decision-making layers.

────────────────────────────────────────
V3 ARCHITECTURAL CHANGE — CIRCULARITY FIX
────────────────────────────────────────

V2 used ZigZag, MACD, Trend Speed, and CVD to classify regime.
All four of those are also direct scoring inputs.

This created a feedback loop:
  - Bullish ZigZag → TRENDING_UP regime
  - TRENDING_UP regime → reduced penalty thresholds
  - ZigZag also scores +25 in the structure layer
  - Score confidence borrowed from the same source as regime

V3 fixes this by deriving regime ONLY from:
  - ATR (volatility/context — not directional)
  - PAVP location (auction zone — not directional scoring input)

ZigZag, MACD, Trend Speed, and CVD are now scoring-only.
They do NOT influence regime classification.

────────────────────────────────────────
V3 REGIME CLASSES (4 + UNCERTAIN)
────────────────────────────────────────

1. TRENDING
   - Price accepted outside Value Area (ABOVE_VA or BELOW_VA)
   - ATR NORMAL or HIGH (but not extreme >90th percentile)
   - ATR expansion not required

2. RANGING
   - Price inside Value Area (INSIDE_VA)
   - ATR LOW or NORMAL (compressed or flat)

3. BREAKOUT
   - Price outside Value Area
   - ATR was compressing, now expanding (compression=False + expansion=True)
   - ATR state NORMAL or HIGH

4. EXHAUSTION
   - ATR percentile rank > 90 (extreme volatility spike)
   - Signals late-move risk regardless of direction

5. UNCERTAIN
   - Cannot classify cleanly from ATR + PAVP alone
   - Apply confidence penalty; require higher threshold to trade

────────────────────────────────────────
INPUTS USED (V3 — non-circular)
────────────────────────────────────────

- ATR: volatility_state, compression, expansion, percentile_rank
- PAVP: value_area_position, value_area_width_pct

NOT used for regime (scoring-only):
- ZigZag Structure
- MACD
- Trend Speed Analyzer
- CVD IQ

────────────────────────────────────────
REGIME → SCORING INTERACTION
────────────────────────────────────────

TRENDING:   No base penalty. Standard scoring thresholds apply.
RANGING:    No base penalty. Location layer weighted more heavily by context.
BREAKOUT:   No base penalty. Momentum signals carry higher weight by context.
EXHAUSTION: No base penalty on score, but risk_state forced to HIGH.
            Calibration note warns of reduced expected edge.
UNCERTAIN:  Base score penalized (-10). Requires score ≥ 80 to produce trade signal.

────────────────────────────────────────
OUTPUT FORMAT
────────────────────────────────────────

Return:
1. Active Regime (one of 5)
2. Derivation sources (ATR state + PAVP position)
3. Regime implication for scoring layer
